This map can be used while working on requirement 2a. It displays all the locations as required in items one through four. Additionally, it displays Eagle Scout projects throughout New Egypt.

How to use:

A layer control widget is located in the upper-right corner of the map. You can toggle the basemap between a dark gray, a street map, and aerial imagery. You can also select which layers will be displayed on the map including goverment buildings, emergency services, hospitals, and parks.
